  3. Para \(A = \begin{pmatrix} 1 \amp -1 \amp 2 \\ -1 \amp 4 \amp 3 \\ 2 \amp 3\amp -2\end{pmatrix}\text{,}\) a transposta é a matriz \(A^T = \begin{pmatrix} 1 \amp -1 \amp 2 \\ -1 \amp 4 \amp 3 \\ 2 \amp 3\amp -2 \end{pmatrix}\text{.}\)
