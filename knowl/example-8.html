Example 1.19.

O sistema

\begin{align*} x - 2y - 3z\amp= 1,\\ 2x + 5y - z \amp= 7,\\ 2x + 3y - 4z \amp= 8, \end{align*}
tem matriz ampliada
\begin{equation*} \left(\begin{array}{ccc|c} 1 \amp -2 \amp -3 \amp 1\\ 2 \amp 5 \amp -1\amp 7\\ 2 \amp 3 \amp -4 \amp 8 \end{array} \right) \sim \cdots \sim \left(\begin{array}{ccc|c} 1 \amp -2 \amp -3 \amp 1\\ 0 \amp 1 \amp 5/9\amp 5/9\\ 0 \amp 0 \amp 1 \amp -19/17 \end{array} \right) \end{equation*}
de modo que \(x_3 = -19/17\text{,}\) \(x_2 = 5/9 +19/17 \times 5/9 = 20/17\) e \(x_1 = 1 + 2\times 20/17 - 3 \times 19/17 = 0.\)
