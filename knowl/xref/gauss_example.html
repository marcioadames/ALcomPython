Example 1.15.

No sistema
\begin{align*} x - 2y - 3z\amp= 1,\\ 2x + 5y - z \amp= 7,\\ 2x + 3y - 4z \amp= 8, \end{align*}
A matriz de coeficientes do sistema:
\begin{equation*} A = \begin{pmatrix} 1 \amp -2 \amp -3\\ 2 \amp 5 \amp -1\\ 2 \amp 3 \amp -4 \end{pmatrix} \end{equation*}
O vetor coluna de constantes:
\begin{equation*} \vec{b} = \begin{pmatrix} 1\\ 7\\ 8 \end{pmatrix} \end{equation*}
A matriz ampliada do sistema:
\begin{equation*} (A | \vec{b}) = \left(\begin{array}{ccc|c} 1 \amp -2 \amp -3 \amp 1\\ 2 \amp 5 \amp -1\amp 7\\ 2 \amp 3 \amp -4 \amp 8 \end{array} \right) \end{equation*}
