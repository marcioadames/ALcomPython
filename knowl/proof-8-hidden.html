  1. \(\vec{0} = 0\vec{v}_1 + 0\vec{v}_2 + \ldots + 0\vec{v}_n \Longrightarrow \vec{0} \in Span(\vec{v}_1, \vec{v}_2, \ldots, \vec{v}_n) \) e \(Span(\vec{v}_1, \vec{v}_2, \ldots, \vec{v}_n) \neq \emptyset.\)

  3. \(\displaystyle \alpha\vec{v} = (\alpha\alpha_1)\vec{v}_1 + (\alpha\alpha_2)\vec{v}_2 + \ldots + (\alpha\alpha_n)\vec{v}_n \Longrightarrow \alpha\vec{v} \in Span(\vec{v}_1, \vec{v}_2, \ldots, \vec{v}_n). \)
