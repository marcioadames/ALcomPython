Example 1.25.

O sistema

\begin{align*} x - 2y - 3z\amp= 1,\\ 2x -2y - 2z \amp= 6, \end{align*}
tem matriz ampliada
\begin{equation*} \left(\begin{array}{ccc|c} 1 \amp -2 \amp -3 \amp 1\\ 2 \amp -2 \amp -2\amp 6 \end{array} \right) \sim \cdots \sim \left(\begin{array}{ccc|c} 1 \amp 0 \amp 1 \amp 5\\ 0 \amp 1 \amp 2\amp 2 \end{array} \right) \end{equation*}
assim o sistema tem infinitas soluções \(y=2-2\alpha,\,\, x= 5 - \alpha, \,\, z = \alpha.\text{.}\)
